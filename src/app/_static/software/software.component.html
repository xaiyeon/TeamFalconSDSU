Software

At Team Falcon we decided to go with the Teensy 3.2 board because of the (MK20DX256) 32-bit ARM Cortex-M4 @72 MHz and additional prototyping on a PIC32MX130F064B. Respectively, the integrated development environment (IDE) used were Arduino Sketch and MPLAB by MicroChip. Both IDEs used the C programming language.

The Code

We use a few libraries for faster prototyping. This gives us more time to program strategies and hasten development process.


Our Strategy Into The Code:

Our team thought of numerous strategies to try and take down our opponents. However, our opponents all had advantages over us and we tried to cover all edge cases (such as in logic) to counter and outperform them. So we devised a way, thanks to using analog sensors, to defining and programming different unique modes for the different opponent's robot.
